Projects

REF. SI-AUTO

AI-Assisted Solution Intent Generation

An end-to-end Python automation triggered the moment a ticket is raised in Azure DevOps. It takes the BRD submitted with the ticket, converts it into Markdown, and uses that to generate the Solution Intent (SI) page and the GFA (Governance Architect Forum) form. It then creates the linked ADO SI work item and sends two emails to the relevant stakeholders — a pre-review meeting invite and an ARF slot-booking mail — closing the loop with no manual steps in between.

PythonAzure DevOpsAIEmail Automation

How it runs end-to-end:

Step 1
Ticket raised in ADO with BRD attached
Step 2
BRD parsed & converted to Markdown
Step 3
SI page & GFA form generated
Step 4
Linked ADO SI work item created
Step 5
Pre-review meeting invite sent
Step 6
ARF slot-booking mail sent
REF. NUTRI-BOT

Personalized Nutrition Guidance Chatbot

NutriSafe is an AI-powered chatbot built with Google Dialogflow that provides personalized nutrition guidance and health risk assessments. The bot uses custom intents and entities to understand user inputs around dietary habits, health conditions, age, BMI, and lifestyle — then delivers tailored nutritional recommendations and flags potential health risks in real time. The system was designed to be conversational and accessible, empowering individuals to take proactive control of their diet without needing to consult a specialist for routine guidance. The chatbot logic was extended into a peer-reviewed research paper published at MP-TEAS 2024.
